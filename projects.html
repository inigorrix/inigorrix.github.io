Knots and games of the tic-tac-toe type

This was my Final Project for my Industrial Design Engineering Degree, it was done with the help and support of Pedro González Manchón. It involved studying the basics of topology and knot theory and it lead to the development of a Python Package and two computer games.

MAFCE stands for "Mechanism for the Application of Forces Controlled Electronically" and it is the machine I designed and built for my Final Project for my Mechanical Engineering Degree. This project was carried out with the help and support of Beatriz Santamaría Fernández and Miguel Berzal Rubio.
